# Liftly research → RepLock slideshow system

**Source:** https://www.tiktok.com/@liftlyapp (reviewed Jul 2026)  
**Account:** Liftly - Gym Tracker · ~2.3k followers · claims 35k+ users

## What they post

Almost every post is a **photo carousel**. Thumbnails use bold white mid-frame text on lifestyle photos.

### Hook formulas (in order of how often they appear)

1. **Bro dialogue** — `Bro: "what's the best feeling in the gym?"` / `Bro: "I'm skipping gym today"` / `Bro: "how are you so big?"`
2. **Emotional / lifestyle line** — `"You've changed"` / `"Skip the gym today"` over couples/flowers
3. **Opinion bait** — `Bulking is SO much harder than cutting` over pizza
4. **Listicle** — `Best gym apps 2026` (pinned, 54.9K views — their top performer)
5. **Soft product UGC** — `I found the best gym app` with real phone-in-hand + app UI

### Sell ratio

~70% vibe/meme (product only in caption or last slide) · ~30% soft product. Soft CTAs: `App: Liftly`, `link in bio`, `>>`.

### Visual rules they follow

- Diverse photos (gym friends, food, flowers, hands, POV) — not one stock gym couple
- No fake device chrome overlays
- Text looks like TikTok Classic (bold white + shadow), mid-frame
- Faceless / UGC-adjacent energy

## What was wrong with our old packs

- Rigid funnel every pack (hook → agitate → SVG phone → Search RepLock)
- Same Unsplash gym couple reused
- Fake phone mock screamed "ad"
- "see slide 4" engagement bait — not native
