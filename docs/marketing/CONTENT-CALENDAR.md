## Daily posting checklist

1. Post at **7–9am** or **6–8pm** (gym crowd)
2. Pin comment: **Search RepLock** → switch to **link in bio** after store live
3. Reply to every comment in first hour
4. Use trending gym phonk / slowed edits (rotate sounds)
5. Cross-post Instagram Reels (same video, no watermark)

## Caption templates (rotate)

**Transformation:** `wasn't a discipline problem. was a phone problem. RepLock locks apps until you earn minutes with real reps 💪`

**Challenge:** `Day {n} of earning my scroll — {exercise} before {app}. who's in?`
