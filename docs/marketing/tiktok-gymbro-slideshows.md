# RepLock Gymbro TikTok Slideshow Pack

**Audience:** 18–30 male, gym-regular, scrolls between sets, knows what "skip leg day" means  
**Format:** 6–7 slide photo carousels (TikTok slideshow)  
**Tone:** Bro-humor, self-aware, meme-adjacent — not corporate, not try-hard  
**Visual tip:** Dark gym footage, mirror selfies, chalk hands, red lighting. White bold text, Impact-style font. Atmosphere + copy first — never dump full app screenshots. At most one small phone mock on the solution slide. Never draw carousel dots (TikTok adds them). No purple AI chrome.

## Production notes

| Element | Spec |
|---------|------|
| Slide count | 6 problem slides + 1 CTA (7 total) |
| Text style | 4–8 words max per slide, ALL CAPS optional on hook |
| Slide 6 pivot | Always introduce RepLock mechanic (earn scroll / apps locked) |
| B-roll ideas | Empty squat rack, rest timer, gym mirror, chalk cloud, locked app screen |
| Posting cadence | 1 slideshow/day for 5 days pre-launch, repost top performer as Story |
| A/B test | Swap final slide between "Search RepLock" vs "Link in bio" after launch |

## Claims checklist (stay honest)

- ✅ "Apps locked until you exercise" — requires OS permissions; blocking is real on supported platforms
- ✅ "Earn scroll time with push-ups, squats, planks" — accurate in-app mechanic
- ❌ Don't claim RepLock builds muscle, fixes sleep, or replaces a training program
- ❌ Don't imply the gym crush will notice you — keep it joke energy
