Privacy Policy

RepLocks · Last updated: July 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how RepLocks ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data when you use the RepLocks mobile application on iOS and Android and related backend services (the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Service.

1. Who we are

RepLocks is the data controller for personal data processed through the Service, except where we use third-party processors listed below.

For privacy questions, support, or to exercise your rights, contact us at RepLockIssue@outlook.com.

Support page: https://rutgermb.github.io/App/legal/support.html.

2. Information we collect

  • Account data: name, email address, and authentication credentials when you register or sign in (via Firebase Authentication).
  • Workout and progress data: exercises completed, amounts/reps, streaks, earned screen-time balance, difficulty settings, and in-app preferences.
  • Blocking configuration: apps you choose to limit, nicknames/display names you assign, daily opening limits, schedules, unlock usage history, and related settings.
  • Subscription data: plan type, trial status, and transaction / entitlement identifiers from Apple In-App Purchase, Google Play Billing, and/or RevenueCat. We do not store full payment card numbers.
  • Technical data: device type, operating system, app version, language, and crash or error diagnostics needed to keep the Service reliable.
  • Communications: messages you send to support.

We do not currently use Firebase Analytics or similar advertising analytics SDKs to build marketing profiles. If that changes, we will update this policy before enabling them.

3. App blocking, Screen Time & device data

Android. With permissions you enable (such as Accessibility and/or Usage Access), RepLocks can block selected apps and, where permitted, read device usage statistics for apps you track. Package names and local enforcement data stay on your device where possible; we store configuration needed to sync your account and show in-app progress.

iOS. RepLocks uses Apple Family Controls / Screen Time APIs (Managed Settings, Device Activity, and related frameworks) that you authorize. Apple presents system UI for selecting apps to shield. Apple restricts what third-party apps can read: we generally cannot obtain the real App Store name or icon of shielded apps for display in our web UI. You may assign a nickname so the app is recognizable in RepLocks. Nicknames and selection tokens may be stored on-device (including in an App Group shared with RepLocks extensions) and synced with your account configuration as needed.

We do not read your passwords, messages, photos, or the full contents of other apps. We do not sell personal data or use blocking data for advertising profiles.

Blocking effectiveness depends on OS permissions, device settings, and Apple/Google platform rules. We do not claim that blocking works in every edge case (for example after OS updates or if permissions are revoked).

4. Push notifications

If you allow notifications, the operating system may grant RepLocks permission to send push or local notifications (for example workout reminders or trial-related notices when those features are enabled). You can change this anytime in system Settings or in the app. Token registration for push delivery may use Apple Push Notification service (APNs) or Google services as provided by the OS and our notification stack. Reminder content may roll out over time; granting permission does not by itself mean every reminder type is already active.

5. How we use your data

  • To provide and operate the Service, including earning screen time through workouts and enforcing limits you configure.
  • To sync progress and settings across devices when you sign in.
  • To process subscriptions, free trials, restores, and entitlement checks (including via RevenueCat).
  • To send service-related messages if you enable notifications.
  • To improve reliability, fix bugs, and understand aggregated product usage.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights and users' safety.

6. Legal bases (EEA/UK)

We process data based on: (a) performance of our contract with you; (b) your consent where required (e.g. optional notifications or certain device permissions); (c) legitimate interests such as security and product improvement; and (d) legal obligations. You may withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.

7. Sharing with third parties

We share data only as needed with trusted service providers, including:

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication and Firestore (or similar) cloud sync for accounts and app state. We do not currently enable Firebase Analytics for advertising.
  • Apple — In-App Purchases, App Store subscription management, Screen Time / Family Controls frameworks, and APNs where applicable.
  • Google — Google Play Billing on Android and related Google mobile services you enable.
  • RevenueCat — subscription status, receipt validation, and entitlement syncing on mobile.
  • Hosting and infrastructure providers that help us run the Service (including GitHub Pages for public legal/support pages).

Providers process data under their own privacy policies and our instructions. We may disclose data if required by law or to protect safety and rights.

8. Retention

We keep account and workout data while your account is active. After you delete your account, we delete or anonymize personal data within a reasonable period, except where we must retain data for legal, tax, or dispute-resolution purposes. Backup copies may persist for a limited time before automatic deletion. On-device App Group / Screen Time configuration may remain until you revoke permissions or uninstall the app.

9. Security

We use technical and organizational measures to protect personal data, including encryption in transit, access controls, and secure authentication. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. Keep your login credentials confidential.

10. Log & diagnostic data

If the app crashes or errors occur, we may collect diagnostic information (device model, OS version, app version, error timestamps). This data is used to improve stability and is not used to identify you beyond your account when you are signed in.

11. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or port your personal data, and to object to certain processing.

You can delete your account in the app under Settings → Delete account. You may also contact RepLockIssue@outlook.com. EEA/UK users may lodge a complaint with their local data protection authority.

12. Children

The Service is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect data from children. Contact us if you believe a child has provided personal data.

13. International transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your own (including by Firebase, Apple, Google, or RevenueCat). Where required, we use appropriate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses approved by the European Commission.

14. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the new version at this URL and update the "Last updated" date. Material changes may require renewed consent where applicable.
