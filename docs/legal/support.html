Common questions

  • Subscriptions & billing — managed through the Apple App Store or Google Play. Cancel anytime in your device subscription settings.
  • Restore purchases — open RepLocks → Pricing → Restore purchases (same Apple ID / Google account).
  • App blocking (iOS) — requires Screen Time / Family Controls permission. Grant access when prompted, then pick apps in the Apps tab.
  • Delete account — Settings → Delete account inside the app.

Response time

We aim to reply within 2 business days. Include your device model, iOS/Android version, and a short description of the issue.
