Common questions

  • Subscriptions & billing — billed through the Apple App Store or Google Play. Price and period (monthly or yearly) are shown before you confirm purchase. Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the period ends.
  • Cancel Pro — open RepLocks → Settings → Manage subscription (opens Apple/Google Customer Center), or cancel in iOS Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions / Google Play → Payments & subscriptions. You keep access until the paid period ends. Deleting the app or account does not cancel billing by itself.
  • Restore purchases — open RepLocks → Pricing → Restore purchases (same Apple ID / Google account).
  • App blocking (iOS) — requires Screen Time / Family Controls permission. Grant access when prompted, then pick apps in the Apps tab.
  • Delete account — open the app → Settings → Delete account, then confirm with your password. This removes your account and synced cloud data (email, workouts, balance, streaks, nicknames, usage history) from our servers. Screen Time / Family Controls selections on the device may remain until you revoke access or uninstall. Cancel store subscriptions separately as above. Need help? Email RepLockIssue@outlook.com from the address on your account.
  • Emails — today we only send transactional account email (e.g. verification and password reset via Firebase). We do not send marketing campaigns. See the Privacy Policy §16 for CAN-SPAM practices if marketing email is added later.
  • How we handle data — see Settings → How we handle data in the app, or the Privacy Policy. We do not sell or share personal information for advertising under CCPA; third parties like Firebase, Apple, Google, and RevenueCat are service providers for running the app.

Response time

We aim to reply within 2 business days. Include your device model, iOS/Android version, and a short description of the issue.
